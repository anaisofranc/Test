Our Focus

The EEA Crosschain Interoperability Working Group is dedicated to researching one of the most imporntant Ethereum challenges. We focus on developing resources - specifications, guidelines and technology - that advance development and adoption of best design and implementation practices.

Our goal is to expand Ethereum interoperability in a secure and scalable way.
